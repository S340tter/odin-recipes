Grant Eggs

Description

This is an amazing egg recipe. It is simple and quick to cook. It will make a wonderful breakfast any day of the week. Try it out for yourself.

Ingredients
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup of diced ham
  • 1 handful of baby spinach
Steps
  1. Cut bell pepper
  2. Mix eggs
  3. Dice ham
  4. Add everything and cook on medium
