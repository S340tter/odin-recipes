Chicken Fajitas

Description

This is an amazing chicken fajita recipe. It is simple and quick to cook. It will make a wonderful lunch or dinner any day of the week. Try it out for yourself.

Ingredients
  • 1/2 tsp Salt and 1/4 tsp Pepper
  • 1/2 tbls of Chilli Powder
  • 1/2 tbls of Cumin
  • 1 tsp of Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp of Paprika
  • 1/2 tsp of Oregano
  • 1.5lbs of Chicken
  • 2-3 Bell Peppers
  • 1 Onion
Steps
  1. Mix the spices
  2. Cover the chicken with spices either on a plate or in a bowl
  3. Cook Chicken on medium heat until done
  4. Cut up veggies while chicken cooks
  5. Remove chicken and put cut veggies in pan and mix
  6. Cut chicken up and put back in pan with veggies
  7. Serve to Hungry People!
